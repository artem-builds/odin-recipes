Lemon Cake

A refreshing and moist lemon cake, perfect for any occasion. The bright citrus flavor combined with a tender crumb makes this cake a delightful treat.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add the milk, lemon juice, and lemon zest.
  5. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  8. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
